About Me

I am an everything visual developer with knowledge and skills to produce a quality product in the new "virtual world" we find ourselves in today.


I have a strong background in Live Event technology and have been involved in major events ranging from large scale corporate events, to world touring concerts, to intimate company meetings. For the last 10 years I have been honing my skill sets in what I consider to be conventional event technologies, such as lighting design and programming, stage design, Media Server Programming, and much more. But I have also developed a unique skill set that is marketable to a wide range of other non-event needs. Some examples of those would be software development, high-level networking infrastructure, 3d modeling and animation, video game development. And I have dabbled in plenty more that makes me an excellent asset to any pipeline. Even if I only work on one specific part of the process, I know where the product comes from and I know where it has to go allowing me to optimize the workflow to speed up development processes for myself and those who handle the product afterwards.
